Banana Pudding

This Krispy Kreme banana pudding makes a surprising switch for vanilla wafers: donuts. And why not! It's every bit the cold, creamy, sweet treat you love, now with a twist.

Ingredients

  • (3.4 ounce) boxes instant French vanilla pudding mix
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 (14 ounce) can condensed milk
  • 16 ounces whipped topping
  • 12 donuts, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 5 bananas, sliced, or more as needed

Steps

  1. Prepare pudding according to package directions and set aside.
  2. Combine cream cheese and condensed milk in a separate bowl and beat using a hand mixer until smooth.
  3. Add pudding to the cream cheese mixture and stir until well combined
  4. Layer half of pudding mixture in a 9x13 dish
  5. Add a layer of bananas and half of donuts
  6. Pour remaining pudding mixture over bananas and donuts
  7. Top with whipped topping and remaining donuts.
  8. Keep refrigerated until ready to serve.
